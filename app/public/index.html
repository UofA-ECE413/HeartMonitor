Heart Track

A project to keep track of your health with our innovative heart monitoring system.

About

Heart Track is a cost-effective, IoT-enabled web application designed to help users monitor their heart rate and blood oxygen saturation levels throughout the day. Leveraging modern IoT technology, Heart Track provides a seamless, customizable, and user-friendly experience for tracking vital health metrics.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Health Monitoring: Uses the MAX30102 sensor to measure heart rate and blood oxygen levels based on user-defined schedules.
  • Customizable Reminders: Configure the frequency and timing of measurements to suit daily routines or healthcare plans.
  • Intuitive Web Application: A responsive design ensures seamless use across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Technology Highlights

  • IoT Integration: Powered by the Particle Argon microcontroller for efficient data collection and transmission.
  • Full-Stack Development: Combines a scalable backend hosted on AWS EC2 with a secure MongoDB database.
  • Ease of Setup: Simple hardware setup using the Particle Argon, MAX30102 sensor, mini breadboard, and jumper wires.
